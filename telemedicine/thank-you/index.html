What happens next

What to Expect.

Confirmation Email

A confirmation email is on its way to you with your appointment details and the secure video link. If you do not see it within a few minutes, check your spam folder. The email comes from our scheduling platform.

Before Your Appointment

A few minutes before your scheduled time, click the video link from your confirmation email. The link works on phones, tablets, and computers without an app download in most cases. Find a calm, well-lit space where you can show your pet to the veterinarian.

During Your Consultation

You will speak directly with one of our emergency veterinarians for 15 to 30 minutes. They will ask questions, look at your pet, and help you decide what to do next. Have any current medication labels and a list of recent symptoms ready if you can.

After Your Consultation

You will receive a written summary of what was discussed and the recommended next steps. If your consultation indicates an in-person visit is needed, your $75 fee is credited toward that visit at our Fort Lauderdale hospital.

Safety net

Need Help Before Your Scheduled Time?

If your pet's condition gets worse before your appointment, do not wait for the scheduled call. Pick up the phone.

If your pet's condition is escalating, call us right now. (954) 772-0420.

We are open weeknights and 24/7 on weekends and holidays. Walk-ins are welcome at our Fort Lauderdale hospital.

Need to Reschedule?

If something comes up and you need to change your appointment time, you can reschedule from the link in your confirmation email or call us at (954) 772-0420 during operating hours. We will work with you to find a time that fits.
